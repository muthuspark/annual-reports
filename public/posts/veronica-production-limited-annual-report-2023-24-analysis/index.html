1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY #

Key Highlights #

  • Revenue: ₹12.91 lakhs in FY2024 (FY2023: ₹0.00 lakhs)
  • Net Profit: ₹0.58 lakhs in FY2024 (FY2023: Loss of ₹508.48 lakhs)
  • EPS: ₹0.00 in FY2024 (FY2023: ₹-0.71)
  • Business Segment: Single segment - ‘Agri Trading Business’
  • Working Capital: Negative in both FY2023 and FY2024
