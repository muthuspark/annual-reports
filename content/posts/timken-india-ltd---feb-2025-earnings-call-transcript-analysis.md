## Earnings Call Transcript Analysis Report
## Financial Performance

### Revenue

Timken India Limited reported its best-ever December quarter (Q3 FY25), with revenues reaching INR 671 crores, a 9.7% year-over-year (YoY) growth. Sequentially, revenue was down due to business cyclicity.

### Profitability

PBT margins stood at 14.6% for Q3 FY25, compared to 14.9% in the prior year period. This was described as a "resilient margin performance" despite an unfavourable product mix, offset by cost leverage. Compared to the previous quarter, margins were down due to adverse deleveraging costs.

### Balance Sheet

The company maintains a debt-free balance sheet with strong cash reserves.

### Revenue Breakdown

Segment-wise revenue contribution for Q3 FY25:

*   Rail: INR 116 crores (17%)
*   Mobile others: INR 139 crores (21%)
*   Distribution: INR 127 crores (19%)
*   Process (stationary equipment): INR 132 crores (20%)
*   Export: INR 152.x crores (23%)
*   Export incentives: 1%
*   Total: INR 671.4 crores

### Year-to-Date Performance

Year-to-date (9 months FY25) revenue was INR 2,208 crores.

Segment-wise YTD contribution:

*   Rail: 21%
*   Mobile: 19%
*   Distribution: 19%
*   Process stationery: 20%
*   Intercompany (Export): 20%
*   Other (export incentives): 1%

## Strategic Initiatives & Business Updates

### Bharuch Greenfield Project

*   The building, utility areas, DG sets, power backups, and 66 kVA line are complete.
*   All machines are inside the building, awaiting HVAC connection for unboxing.
*   Initial PPAPs (Production Part Approval Process) are targeted by the end of March 2025.
*   The plant will produce Spherical Roller Bearings (SRB) and Cylindrical Roller Bearings (CRB) for the first time in India for Timken, up to 400mm for SRB and 300mm for CRB.
*   Capacity Utilization Target: 50-60% in the first year (FY26), 70-75% in the second year, and full capacity by the third year.
*   The major focus for the new plant is exports, followed by import substitution and new domestic customers.

### Parent Company Strategy & Localization

*   A new seasoned leader at the parent company (The Timken Company) emphasizes sourcing from "best-cost countries."
*   Strategy includes "local for local, and local for local plus global" and integration on the drivetrain.

### Future Localization Opportunities

Future localization opportunities in India for other Timken product lines include:

*   **Industrial Motion:** Lubrication systems, couplings, chains.
